    Tighnari

    VA : KOBAYASHI Sanae

    An Avidya Forest Watcher and botanical scholar who graduated from Amurta. He leads a fruitful life of patrolling the rainforest, protecting the ecology, and lecturing fools every day.

    Nahida

    VA : TAMURA Yukari

    Lesser Lord Kusanali dwells deep in the Sanctuary of Surasthana, and has never really been in limelight, nor has she even been mentioned much. Her burden is heavy, but though she may experience loneliness, and though darkness is all she sees before her, she will not stop moving forward

    Dehya

    VA : FUKUHARA Ayaka

    A member of “The Eremites,” a loosely-organized mercenary organization. She is brave, powerful, and enjoys an excellent reputation among mercenaries.
